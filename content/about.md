# About Me

I am a **Scientific and Technologic Officer** at the **University of Naples Federico II**, working within the Department of Agricultural Sciences. My role bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and practical technological implementation in the agricultural sector.

## Professional Focus

My work centres on supporting research activities through the management and development of scientific instrumentation, data analysis pipelines, and laboratory technologies. I collaborate with researchers across multiple disciplines to ensure that technical infrastructure meets the evolving demands of modern agricultural science.

## Areas of Expertise

I specialise in the integration of digital technologies within agricultural research environments, including precision agriculture tools, environmental monitoring systems, and data management solutions. My technical background enables me to provide comprehensive support for experimental design, instrument calibration, and the implementation of innovative methodologies.

## Philosophy

I believe that robust scientific advancement requires equally robust technical foundations. By ensuring that researchers have access to well-maintained, properly configured instrumentation and efficient data workflows, I contribute to the broader mission of advancing agricultural knowledge and sustainability.
